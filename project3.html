Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Anguste-Domnique Ingres

The potrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Lourve. It is the third of three potraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year

Date: 1806

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm

Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
